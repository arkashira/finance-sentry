# Partner Targets
## Introduction
The finance-sentry platform aims to provide seamless transaction monitoring and resolution for financial service companies. To enhance its capabilities and expand its reach, we have identified key partners for integration. This document outlines our partner integration roadmap, highlighting 7 strategic SaaS/API partners, their free-tier limits, integration effort, and the value-add they bring to our users.

## Partner Integration Roadmap

1. **Plaid**
	* Free-tier limit: 100 items (accounts, transactions, etc.) per month
	* Integration effort: Medium (M)
	* Value-add: Simplifies user onboarding by providing secure and reliable financial data connectivity, solving the user job of "easily linking financial accounts"
	* Affiliate/Revenue-share: Yes, Plaid offers a revenue-sharing model for referrals
2. **Stripe**
	* Free-tier limit: No free tier, but competitive pricing for transactions
	* Integration effort: Small (S)
	* Value-add: Enables finance-sentry to offer seamless payment processing and transaction monitoring, solving the user job of "streamlining payment operations"
	* Affiliate/Revenue-share: Yes, Stripe offers a revenue-sharing model for partners
3. **Xero**
	* Free-tier limit: Limited to 5 invoices and 20 transactions per month
	* Integration effort: Medium (M)
	* Value-add: Provides accounting and bookkeeping capabilities, solving the user job of "accurately tracking financial transactions"
	* Affiliate/Revenue-share: Yes, Xero offers an affiliate program with revenue-sharing
4. **QuickBooks**
	* Free-tier limit: 30-day free trial, then subscription-based
	* Integration effort: Large (L)
	* Value-add: Offers comprehensive accounting and financial management tools, solving the user job of "managing financial operations"
	* Affiliate/Revenue-share: Yes, QuickBooks offers an affiliate program with revenue-sharing
5. **PayPal**
	* Free-tier limit: No free tier, but competitive pricing for transactions
	* Integration effort: Small (S)
	* Value-add: Enables finance-sentry to offer diverse payment options and transaction monitoring, solving the user job of "expanding payment channels"
	* Affiliate/Revenue-share: Yes, PayPal offers a revenue-sharing model for partners
6. **Bank of America (API)** 
	* Free-tier limit: Varies based on the specific API and usage
	* Integration effort: Large (L)
	* Value-add: Provides direct access to banking services and transaction data, solving the user job of "securely accessing financial data"
	* Affiliate/Revenue-share: Potential for revenue-sharing, but requires direct negotiation
7. **Square**
	* Free-tier limit: No free tier, but competitive pricing for transactions
	* Integration effort: Small (S)
	* Value-add: Enables finance-sentry to offer point-of-sale and transaction monitoring capabilities, solving the user job of "streamlining retail operations"
	* Affiliate/Revenue-share: Yes, Square offers a revenue-sharing model for partners

## Prioritization
Our prioritization strategy focuses on partners that offer affiliate or revenue-sharing models, as these provide direct financial benefits. We also consider the integration effort and the value-add each partner brings to our users. By integrating with these strategic partners, finance-sentry can enhance its capabilities, expand its reach, and provide more comprehensive solutions to financial service companies.
