# Marketing Plan
## Positioning
Finance-sentry: Real-time transaction monitoring and automated issue resolution for financial service companies.
Alternative positionings:
1. Finance-sentry: AI-powered transaction tracking and resolution for financial institutions.
2. Finance-sentry: Automated transaction monitoring and remediation for financial service operations teams.
3. Finance-sentry: Proactive transaction risk management and resolution for financial companies.

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
1. **Alex Chen**: Financial Operations Manager at a mid-sized bank, responsible for daily transaction processing, with a budget of $100,000 for transaction monitoring tools.
	* Daily job: Oversees transaction processing, identifies and resolves issues, and ensures compliance with regulatory requirements.
2. **Maya Patel**: Head of Risk Management at a fintech company, responsible for mitigating transaction risks, with a budget of $200,000 for risk management solutions.
	* Daily job: Develops and implements risk management strategies, monitors transaction activity, and collaborates with cross-functional teams to resolve issues.
3. **Ethan Kim**: IT Director at a large financial services firm, responsible for implementing and maintaining transaction processing systems, with a budget of $500,000 for IT infrastructure and tools.
	* Daily job: Oversees IT operations, ensures system uptime and security, and evaluates new technologies to improve transaction processing efficiency.

## Channels
Top 3 channels with estimated Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):
1. **LinkedIn Ads**: Targeted ads to financial service professionals, estimated CAC: $500.
2. **Industry Conferences**: Sponsorship and speaking opportunities at financial services conferences, estimated CAC: $2,000.
3. **Content Marketing**: Creation and promotion of informative content (blog posts, whitepapers, webinars) on transaction monitoring and risk management, estimated CAC: $300.

## Content Cadence
Week-by-week content plan:
Week 1-2: Introduction to finance-sentry and its benefits
Week 3-4: Transaction monitoring and risk management best practices
Week 5-6: Case studies and success stories
Week 7-8: Product demos and feature highlights
Week 9-10: Industry trends and thought leadership pieces
Week 11-12: Customer testimonials and user-generated content

## Launch Milestones
Key launch milestones:
* **D-30**: Finalize marketing plan, launch content calendar, and begin LinkedIn ad campaign.
* **D-0**: Launch finance-sentry platform, announce product availability, and start industry conference sponsorships.
* **D+30**: Analyze early adoption metrics, adjust marketing strategy, and optimize content and ad targeting.
* **D+90**: Evaluate launch success, assess customer acquisition costs, and plan for future growth and expansion.

## Success Metrics
Key performance indicators:
* **DAU (Daily Active Users)**: 500 users by D+90.
* **$MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)**: $250,000 by D+90, with a growth rate of 20% MoM.
