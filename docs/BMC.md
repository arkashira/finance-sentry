# Business Model Canvas – **Finance‑Sentry**

| **Section** | **Details** |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Value Proposition** | • **Real‑time transaction monitoring** – instant alerts on suspicious or non‑compliant activity.<br>• **Automated issue resolution** – rule‑based workflows that auto‑flag, triage, and resolve common compliance incidents.<br>• **Seamless integration** – plug‑and‑play connectors for core banking, payment gateways, and AML/KYC engines.<br>• **Audit‑ready reporting** – fully auditable logs, dashboards, and exportable reports for regulators and internal teams.<br>• **Scalable, low‑latency** – built on the vLLM inference engine for fast, cost‑effective processing of high‑volume feeds. |
| **Customer Segments** | • **Retail banks & credit unions** – need to meet AML/KYC, fraud detection, and regulatory reporting.<br>• **FinTech payment processors** – require real‑time fraud monitoring and dispute resolution.<br>• **Digital‑only banks & neobanks** – high‑volume, low‑margin operations that benefit from automated compliance.<br>• **Regulatory bodies & auditors** – use exported audit logs for compliance verification. |
| **Channels** | • **Direct sales** – dedicated account managers targeting mid‑to‑large financial institutions.<br>• **Partner ecosystem** – integrations with core banking platforms (e.g., FIS, Temenos) and AML vendors (e.g., NICE, FICO).<br>• **Online marketing** – webinars, whitepapers, and SEO‑optimized content on compliance best practices.<br>• **Industry events** – sponsorship and speaking slots at AML, fintech, and banking conferences. |
| **Revenue Streams** | • **Subscription (SaaS)** – tiered plans (Starter, Professional, Enterprise) based on transaction volume and feature set.<br>• **Professional services** – implementation, customization, and compliance consulting.<br>• **Marketplace add‑ons** – optional connectors and advanced analytics modules sold as add‑ons.<br>• **Data‑as‑a‑Service** – anonymized transaction patterns sold to research firms (subject to regulatory approval). |
| **Cost Structure** | • **Infrastructure** – cloud compute (vLLM inference nodes), storage, and networking.<br>• **Development** – engineering, QA, and product management for continuous feature releases.<br>• **Compliance & legal** – regulatory audits, data privacy compliance, and licensing fees.<br>• **Sales & marketing** – account executives, marketing campaigns, and partner enablement.<br>• **Customer support** – 24/7 helpdesk and onboarding specialists. |
| **Key Resources** | • **Technology stack** – vLLM inference engine, SGLang for structured generation, and Axentx’s shared pgvector knowledge base.<br>• **Data assets** – ~7M auto‑generated pairs, ~6M instruction‑response pairs, and ~3M message pairs for training and fine‑tuning.<br>• **Talent** – senior product/engineering leads, data scientists, compliance experts.<br>• **Partnerships** – core banking vendors, AML/KYC solution providers, regulatory bodies. |
| **Key Activities** | • **Product development** – building real‑time monitoring pipelines, automated workflow engines, and audit dashboards.<br>• **Data curation & model training** – leveraging Axentx’s datasets to train domain‑specific compliance models.<br>• **Integration & deployment** – creating APIs, SDKs, and connectors for partner ecosystems.<br>• **Compliance & security** – maintaining certifications (PCI‑DSS, ISO 27001) and staying ahead of regulatory changes.<br>• **Customer success** – onboarding, training, and support to ensure high retention. |
| **Key Partners** | • **Core banking platform vendors** (FIS, Temenos, Avaloq) – for seamless data ingestion.<br>• **AML/KYC solution providers** (NICE, FICO, Refinitiv) – to enrich rule sets and threat intelligence.<br>• **Cloud providers** (AWS, GCP, Azure) – for scalable infrastructure.<br>• **Regulatory bodies** (FINRA, FCA, MAS) – for compliance validation and joint certification programs.<br>• **Industry associations** (FinTech Alliance, AML Council) – for thought leadership and market reach. |
